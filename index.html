About Dr. David C. Chao

At Fort Bend Pulmonology and Sleep Medicine, we believe that respiration is the essence of life, operating in each and every cell of your body. Dr. David C. Chao, M.D., FCCP, brings a wealth of expertise and experience to his practice, dedicated to providing exceptional care in the fields of pulmonology, somnology, and critical care medicine.

Dr. Chao is Board Certified by the American Board of Internal Medicine (ABIM) in three specialties: Pulmonology, Somnology (Sleep Medicine), and Critical Care Medicine. With a commitment to excellence and a patient-centered approach, Dr. Chao ensures each patient receives comprehensive evaluation and personalized management tailored to their unique needs.

Services Offered

Dr. Chao specializes in the comprehensive evaluation and management of various respiratory and sleep disorders, including but not limited to:

  • Shortness of breath
  • Cough
  • Abnormal breathing sounds
  • Sleep disturbance
  • Abnormal sleepiness
  • Chest pain
  • Abnormal X-ray/CT findings

At Fort Bend Pulmonology and Sleep Medicine, we are committed to helping you breathe easier and sleep better. Trust Dr. Chao for expert diagnosis, personalized treatment plans, and compassionate care.

Accomplishments

Dr. David C. Chao, M.D., FCCP, who speaks Chinese, has been in practice since 1988. He brings a wealth of knowledge and accomplishments to his practice:

  • Former Assistant Clinical Professor at the University of California-Los Angeles (UCLA) School of Medicine
  • Former Clinical Assistant Professor at the University of Southern California (USC) School of Medicine
  • Clinical Faculty at the Sam Houston State University College of Osteopathic Medicine
  • Medical Director of the Sleep Medicine Lab, Intensive Care Unit (ICU), and Respiratory Care Department
  • Author of over 40 publications (search "Chaodc" in PubMed)
  • Fellow of the American College of Chest Physicians

Patients describe Dr. Chao as very knowledgeable and attentive. They value his thoroughness, friendliness and kindness, as well as his ability to answer questions and explain conditions.
